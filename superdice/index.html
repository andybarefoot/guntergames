Super Dice

About Super Dice

Super Dice is a simple game that has 2 modes:

Farkle: Play Farkle with up to 6 players. The app takes care of the scoring and rules. You can choose the target score and the number of players.

Just Dice: Choose the number of dice you need and app acts as a simple random roller. Ideal for playing dice games when you don't have physical dice to hand or if you have a board game that is missing dice.
